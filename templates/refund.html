Refund Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Thank you for purchasing digital assets from Wild Bill Pro Plans. Please read our returns and cancellation guidelines carefully before making a purchase.

1. Digital Downloads Purchase Terms

Due to the immediate access nature of our digital artwork bundles, assets, and ZIP collections, all sales are considered final once secure delivery links have been generated or accessed. Because digital files cannot be physically returned, we do not issue routine refunds after a product has been downloaded.

2. Technical Issues & Damaged Archives

We stand behind the quality of our graphic files. You are eligible for an exchange, file remediation, or a full refund under the following conditions:

  • The downloaded ZIP file archive is corrupted, unreadable, or defective.
  • The file inventory is missing essential advertised file layers or extensions (such as missing SVG or PNG variants).
  • You receive an completely incorrect asset collection bundle due to a server delivery fault.

To request a resolution for a broken file, you must contact our team within fourteen (14) days of your initial marketplace transaction.

3. How to Submit a Ticket

If you experience delivery script failures, credential issues, or archive issues, please navigate directly to our technical support contact console. Be sure to provide your transaction email address and asset SKU information so our team can resolve the issue immediately.

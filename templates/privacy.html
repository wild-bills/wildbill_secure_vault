Privacy Policy

At WildBill's Vault, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled when using our store platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a purchase or access your digital delivery keys, we collect standard operational data necessary to process your transaction:

  • Email address for secure link generation and transaction receipt delivery.
  • Payment transaction data passed securely via our payment gateways.
  • Standard logging variables required to prevent bandwidth link-sharing fraud.

2. Secure Media Storage & Downloads

All download packages are hosted inside encrypted cloud storage repositories. The download engine generates private, temporary tokens that auto-expire after 15 minutes to guarantee data security and protect file access paths from unauthorized discovery.

3. Third-Party Frameworks

We do not sell, trade, or share your contact or delivery metrics with external data brokers. Information is only securely read by our application hosting network (Render) and our payment checkout layers (NexaPay) to fulfill your assets.
